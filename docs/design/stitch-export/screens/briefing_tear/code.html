Objectives

The primary directive for this capsule launch is to establish a striking, high-contrast narrative that aligns with the brand's shift towards brutalist minimalism. Content must avoid traditional warmth, favoring a cool, authoritative tone.

Key performance indicators focus heavily on engagement depth rather than raw reach. We are targeting high-intent fashion connoisseurs; thus, the visual language must be impeccably curated and deeply editorial.

Requirements

camera_alt

Visuals

Minimum 4 high-res stills. Hard flash, minimal retouching, concrete or stark white backgrounds.

format_align_left

Copywriting

Dry, descriptive, and objective tone. Maximum 40 words per post. Include designated hashtags.

movie

Video Asset (Optional)

A 15-second ambient b-roll clip capturing texture. No voiceover, raw audio only. Must be shot in 4k ProRes.
